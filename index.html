About Me

Supriya Shrinate was born and brought up in Lucknow, the capital city of the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. She attended Loreto Convent in Lucknow for her primary and secondary education. After completing her schooling, she went on to pursue higher education at the University of Delhi, where she obtained a Bachelor's degree in History. She then completed a Master's degree in History from the same university. She was interested in journalism and communication. She went on to pursue a career in journalism and has worked with several prominent news organizations over the years.

My Legacy

Harsh Vardhan Ji was born on December 1 1947, in the city of Gorakhpur, located in the state of Uttar Pradesh. He was born into a family with a political background. His father, Sri Rampyare Lal Ji, was also a prominent political leader who served as a member of the Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly. Harsh Vardhan Ji received his early education at the Lucknow Sainik School, a prestigious military school in Uttar Pradesh, India. After completing his schooling, he went on to earn a Bachelor of Arts degree from Lucknow University, also located in Uttar Pradesh.
